Support PeaZip project development

PeaZip started in 2006 as a spare-time project for a cross-platform, Open Source frontend for various command line compression and file management technologies.

You can donate to PeaZip project from paypal.me/peazip

The project employs various third parts technologies, and I'll be glad if you consider about donation to respective Authors: 7-Zip / p7zip, PAQ, FreeArc, and others are all great Open Source projects and deserves all possible support to keep up with development of their excellent programs.
But in first and foremost place a big thank you goes to Lazarus and FreePascal team, providing the cross-platform IDE that makes programming PeaZip possible, easy to test and maintain, and makes possible to port it to many different hardware and software platforms.

Appeal for donation to charitable organizations


Over the time, PeaZip project gained some focus, and it gave to me the opportunity to turn the project's perceived value into something even more positive: I'll be very glad if you consider to donate to charitable organizations which need and deserve your help far more than myself.

Donations to following organizations are completely unrelated to donations to PeaZip project, and each link brings directly to donation / fund raising pages of the official web domains of listed organizations.

NOTE: in many countries charity donations to non-profit organizations can be deducted from taxable income.

FAO


The Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) is a specialised agency of the United Nations that leads international efforts to defeat hunger. Please visit FAO domain for getting informed about different ways to get involved and fund FAO initiatives.

UNICEF


The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) provides long-term humanitarian and developmental assistance to children and mothers in developing countries. Support UNICEF See also: UNICEF USA

UNESCO


United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations to contribute to peace and security by promoting international collaboration through education, science, and culture. UNESCO Online Donations See also: UNESCO World Heritage

Open Source Initiative

PeaZip is Free Software and uses third party technologies also released as Free Software: nothing of this would probably exist without those third parts technologies, and development and testing tools, in a word without the ongoing efforts of those organizations to support the Free Software movement as a way to protect user's and developer's rights to freely share works, experiences and ideas.

Please note that list doesn't aim to be exhaustive, nor to give a sort of "ranking" of merit between various charity organizations and causes.
Fund rising is vital to many charity organizations: if you had visited that page to give an economic contribution to PeaZip project, feel free to consider, instead, to donate that amount to any other organization or cause you believe in, listed or not on this page.
Thank you very much!
